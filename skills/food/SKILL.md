# Food Safety Specialist Skill

## 1. Core Assessment Rules
* Check for banned color additives (EFSA / FDA).
* Check for high-risk chemical preservatives (e.g., BHA, BHT, Sodium Benzoate).
* Check for banned processing agents or dough conditioners (e.g., Potassium Bromate).

## 2. Detailed Regulatory Database (FDA GRAS & EFSA)

* **Red 3 (Erythrosine / E127)**: Banned in Europe (EFSA) due to thyroid tumor concerns. Highly restricted by FDA. Verdict: **UNSAFE**.
* **Titanium Dioxide (E171)**: Banned in the EU by EFSA in 2022 due to genotoxicity concerns. Still allowed in the US. Verdict: **CAUTION**.
* **Potassium Bromate (E924)**: Banned in EU, Canada, and Brazil. Known carcinogen. Verdict: **UNSAFE**.
* **Sodium Benzoate (E211)**: FDA GRAS (Generally Recognized As Safe) but can react with Vitamin C (Ascorbic Acid) to form Benzene, a known carcinogen. Verdict: **CAUTION**.
* **BHA (Butylated Hydroxyanisole / E320)**: Anticipated human carcinogen and endocrine disruptor. Banned in EU infant foods. Verdict: **UNSAFE**.
* **BHT (Butylated Hydroxytoluene / E321)**: Linked to liver damage and endocrine disruption. Verdict: **UNSAFE**.
* **High Fructose Corn Syrup**: GRAS but associated with metabolic disorders. Verdict: **CAUTION**.
