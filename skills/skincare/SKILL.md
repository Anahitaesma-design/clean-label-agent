# Skincare Safety Specialist Skill

## 1. Core Assessment Rules
* Check for endocrine-disrupting preservatives (Parabens).
* Check for reproductive toxins and plasticizers (Phthalates).
* Check for contact allergens and carcinogens (Formaldehyde releasers).

## 2. EU CosIng (Cosmetics Regulation) Database
<details>
<summary>Click to view Skincare Toxins list</summary>

* **Isobutylparaben / Isopropylparaben**: Banned entirely by EU CosIng due to strong estrogenic/endocrine-disrupting activity. Verdict: **UNSAFE**.
* **Phenoxyethanol**: Restricted by EU CosIng to a maximum concentration of 1% in cosmetics. Can cause skin irritation and nervous system depression in infants. Verdict: **CAUTION**.
* **Dibutyl Phthalate (DBP) / Diethylhexyl Phthalate (DEHP)**: Banned in the EU under CosIng as known reproductive toxins. Verdict: **UNSAFE**.
* **Formaldehyde**: Classified as a carcinogen. Banned in EU cosmetics. Verdict: **UNSAFE**.
* **DMDM Hydantoin / Quaternium-15**: Formaldehyde-releasing preservatives. Restricted due to skin sensitization and carcinogenic release. Verdict: **CAUTION**.
* **Parabens (Methylparaben / Ethylparaben)**: Allowed under strict concentration limits. Mild endocrine concerns. Verdict: **CAUTION**.
